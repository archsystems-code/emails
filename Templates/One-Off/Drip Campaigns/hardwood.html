Technology and creativity intersect in sustainability engineered, solid and reclaimed ASI Hardwood high performance flooring. These precision-milled planks are featured in light to dark colorations and include domestic or exotic species and finishes varying from classic to fumed, smoked and weathered textures all in a range of sizes and thicknesses. The ASI Hardwood collections are eco-friendly and Lacy Act Compliant, with many offered as FSC® Certified, CARB (California Air Resources Board) Compliant and Made in the USA.
